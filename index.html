Expected STM32 format

The parser expects your existing CSV line format:
pH,turbidity_NTU,temperature_C,do_mgL\r\n 7.02,12.3,22.48,8.31
That maps to pH, NTU, temperature °C, dissolved oxygen mg/L.

Offline/PWA notes

Once opened once online, the shell of this app can be launched offline. Live USB serial still requires Chrome/Chromium plus local device permissions.
